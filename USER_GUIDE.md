## For Dealers and Sales Staff: Driver Selection

### How to Select Drivers with Preferences

When creating a new delivery request, you'll now see an enhanced driver selection interface:

1. **Click the "Select Driver" button** instead of using a dropdown
2. **Search for drivers** by name or vehicle type using the search bar
3. **Sort drivers** by your preference:
   - **My Preferences**: Drivers you've used and rated highly appear first
   - **Top Performers**: Drivers with best completion rates and on-time delivery
   - **Recently Used**: Drivers you've worked with most recently

### Understanding Driver Metrics

- **Completed Deliveries**: Total successful deliveries for your dealership
- **Success Rate**: Percentage of on-time, completed deliveries
- **Last Used**: When you last worked with this driver
- **Usage Count**: How many times you've selected this driver

## For Drivers: Dealership Organization

### Working with Multiple Dealerships

When you work for multiple dealerships, SwapRunn helps you stay organized:

1. **Color-Coded Requests**: Each dealership has a unique color
   - Left border of request cards matches dealership color
   - Dealership badge uses the same color
   - Accept button uses dealership's color

2. **Dealership Filter Bar**:
   - Appears at the top when you work with 2+ dealerships
   - Shows all dealerships with active request counts
   - Click a dealership to see only their requests
   - Click "All Dealerships" to see everything

3. **Visual Organization**:
   - üè¢ Building icon next to dealership name
   - üïí Time indicator shows how long ago request was posted
   - Color-coded throughout for quick identification

### Tips for Efficiency

- Use the dealership filter to focus on one client at a time
- Colors help you quickly spot priority dealerships
- Request counts show where the most work is available
- Filter persists as you navigate, maintaining your view

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What if I don't rate a driver?
**A:** No problem! Rating is completely optional. The system will still track usage patterns.

### Q: Can I change my rating later?
**A:** Yes! Rating the same driver again updates your preference level.

### Q: What if a driver works for multiple dealerships I use?
**A:** Your preferences are tracked per dealership, so you can rate the same driver differently at different dealerships.

### Q: How many dealerships will show different colors?
**A:** The system supports 10 distinct colors that rotate. All dealerships get a unique color within your working group.

### Q: Can I turn off the dealership filter?
**A:** The filter only appears when you work with 2+ dealerships. If you work with just one, you won't see it.

### Q: Will this work offline?
**A:** The app requires internet connection to load driver data and submit preferences, but gracefully handles offline scenarios.
